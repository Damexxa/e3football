About Us

E3 Football Agency is an agency that was established and inspired by the desire to support talented and passionate footballers in achieving their dreams. We believe that many players lack opportunities, and our mission is to bridge that gap.

With strong partnerships across Europe, Asia, Africa, and the USA, we provide players with exposure to professional clubs, scouts, and agents who can take them to the next level.

E3 Football Agency runs in 3 categories:
1. Players who want to learn (Ages 8–14).
2. Players with skills but need the basics to succeed (Ages 12–16).
3. Players ready for real opportunities (Ages 15–25).

By God’s grace, E3 Football Agency already has players abroad living their dreams, and with our dedicated team, more talents will rise from here.
